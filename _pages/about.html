Prior to joining Berkeley, I received my B.S. degree in Applied Mathematics and Industrial Engineering & Management Science from Northwestern University in 2020.

Research Interests

My research centers on pricing problems in the context of strategic consumer behaviors, particularly reference effects. I seek to provide both theoretical guarantees and practical insights with techniques from online learning, game theory, and sequential decision-making.
